About Me

My name is Nalani Kennedy and I am 20 years old. I grew up a military brat, typically moving every three to four years. I was born in Nebraska, but my family moved before I could even talk or walk.
From when I was a baby to now, I've spent ten total years living in Japan. It is the closest thing I have to a hometown. I spent the whole of my adolescence there and I know I'll never forget it. Even now,I am back to living in the states, but it still feels like I left my heart there across the pacific.
My goal with this blog is to share my years of adventures and memories and use my experience to teach people about Japan, as well as encourage and guide those looking to visit. I hope to share my enormous love for the country and inspire others to explore and embrace something new.
